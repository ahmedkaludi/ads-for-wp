ADS for WP is a one way stop for integrating Ads on site.

== Description ==
ADS for WP is the Successor of the ADVANCED AMP ADS plugin with all goodness of it but much rich with features.

= Features: =

* Unlimited Ads
* Shortcodes Available
* Adsense Support
* DoubleClick Support
* Custom Ad HTML support
* AMP Compatibility: AmpforWp Seamless integration & AMP by Automattic..
* Post Specific Ads Control
* 12 Fixed Ad positions (below header, after related post, etc)
* Unlimited InContent Ads: After nth paragraph
* Sticky Ads Support
* AMP Auto Ads Support
* Link Ads Support
* 13 different Ad dimensions along with Custom dimension support
* Parallax effect ( AMP flying carpet Ad)
* Optimized Ads
